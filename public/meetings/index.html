Meetings

Join us for weekly sessions, topic reviews, and strategy discussions.

In an effort to provide our students with the most resources our club programming hosts three different meetings a week:

  • 2 Office Hours/week
  • 1 Seminar/week

At Office Hours we set up for an hour allowing a space for members to come ask questions, review topics, review practice questions
